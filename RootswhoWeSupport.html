Roots: Who We Support

Our Commitment

This page will provide information about the specific groups of Indigenous online learners that we aim to support through our resources and services.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive online space that empowers Indigenous students to succeed academically while staying connected to their cultural roots. We aim to provide resources, guidance, and community connections that foster a sense of belonging and promote academic success.
