About Us

The Indigenous Student Resource Hub is a Welcoming space that gathers resources, tools, wellness guides, funding resources and networks to help support Indigneous Online Learners

Mission Statement

The mission of this hub is to support Indigenous online learners by creating a space that encourages grounding, connection, and growth. We honour the importance of learning in a good way — staying rooted in culture, community, and land even while studying in digital spaces. This hub brings together academic support, wellness practices, and community resources to help students feel understood, connected, and supported on their learning journey!

Who This Hub Supports

  • Indigenous student learnbing online
  • Parents Balancing School and Family
  • Students returning to school
  • Anyone Seeking cultrually grounded supports
