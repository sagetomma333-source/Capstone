Balance: Wellness and Grounding

Grounding for Online Learners

Practical wellness practices, grounding techniques, and culturally relevant supports to help maintain balance while learning online.

Grounding practices help Indigenous learners stay connected to land, culture, and self while navigating online spaces.

This page will provide information and resources for maintaining wellness and grounding as an Indigenous online learner.
